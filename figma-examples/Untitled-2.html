Annotations just got an upgrade

No more toggling between modes—annotations and measurements can now be created in design mode. Plus, organize your annotations with color-coded categories for easier file navigation. Handoff has never been easier.

Learn more →

Now presenting Figma Slides

Figma Slides is now included on all plans, and comes with four new updates. You can now animate objects, better integrate video, add page numbers, and easily import or export to .PPTX. Look at you, all decked out.

Learn more →

...and a whole lot more

Check your color contrast accessibility

The color picker tool will now check the contrast of your colors against the background to ensure it meets AA or AAA standards.

Reorder your collections and modes

Until now, you had to delete and recreate modes or collections to reorganize them—now you can simply drag and drop without breaking any dependencies.

Collapse all layers with a click

Collapse all layers, directly from the layers panel. It’s a small but mighty update to make your workflow a little tidier.
